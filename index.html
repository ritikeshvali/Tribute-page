Edsger Wybe Dijkstra

The man who gave the shortest way out.

The timeline of Edsgar Dijkstra's Life.

  • 1930: Born in Rotterdam, Netherlands.
  • 1948: Graduated school.
  • 1957: Married Maria C. Debets .
  • 1959: Received his PhD from the University of Amsterdam.
  • 1959: Published a paper which instigated the development of Dijkstra's Algorithm.
  • 1962: Became a professor in the Mathematics Department at the Eindhoven University of Technology.
  • 1972: Received the ACM Turing Award.
  • 1973: Joined Burroughs Corporation as a Research Fellow.
  • 1984: Moved to University of Texas at Austin.
  • 1999: Retired.
  • 2002: Died.
